ABOUT Sunflower Community

Welcome to the Sunflower Community Orphans Support Project website, where we are dedicated to improving the lives of orphaned children in Kenya. Our mission is to provide essential support and resources to these vulnerable children, helping them to grow and thrive in a safe and nurturing environment.

We believe that every child deserves the opportunity to reach their full potential, and we are committed to making that a reality for the children in our care.

Our team is made up of individuals with a deep commitment to improving the lives of orphaned children. We are working to provide basic needs such as food, shelter, and education to these children, as well as emotional and psychological support to help them cope with their loss.

We would not be able to do this work without the support of people like you. There are many ways to get involved and make a difference in the lives of these children.

You can donate, sponsor a child, volunteer your time, or spread awareness about our project. Every little bit helps and we are grateful for any support that you can provide. If you would like to make a donation, please visit our donation page or contact us for more information on how to become a sponsor. Together, we can make a difference in the lives of orphaned children in Kenya.
